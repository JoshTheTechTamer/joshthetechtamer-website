About Josh Sargent

Howdy there!

First off, thanks for stopping by my website and considering my services. I've been helping folks with their computer troubles for over 20 years—since I was a kid in the '90s. I'm happy to announce that my tech expertise is no longer exclusive to my in-laws… I'm here to help you too!

Now, I'll be the first to admit—I am NO plumber. The second a pipe springs a leak in my house, I've gotta call a guy to handle it. But when it comes to tech problems? I'm the guy you call. If you're stuck with a device that won't cooperate and have no clue where to start, I can get you reconnected and running smoothly.

I have a bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering Technology from Hamilton Tech in Davenport, IA, along with a Network Engineering diploma from MCC in Muscatine, IA (specializing in Cisco systems). My expertise extends to web design, marketing, and advertising sales, helping small businesses with their digital presence. Currently, I work in sales at a corporate mobile carrier, keeping me up to speed on the latest tech trends and products.

I have tons of hands-on experience, and I know how frustrating it is when your device just… stops working. I also understand the sheer panic of clicking something in a settings menu and watching everything on-screen go haywire. No worries—your new pal Josh is only a free text away from answers (563) 261-0200. I'll get your devices working again and set up the way you want them!
