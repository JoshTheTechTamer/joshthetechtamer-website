About Josh Sargent

For over 20 years, I've been dedicated to helping people overcome their technology challenges with patience, understanding, and expertise. As a disabled Army veteran who served as a food service specialist from 2008 to 2009, I've developed a unique approach to making technology accessible and less intimidating for everyone.

My educational foundation includes a Bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering Technology from Hamilton Tech in Davenport, Iowa, complemented by a diploma in Network Engineering with Cisco Systems from MCC Eastern Iowa Community College. This technical background allows me to tackle complex issues while explaining solutions in simple, understandable terms.

I specialize in assisting seniors and individuals who find technology challenging but necessary in today's digital world. Whether you've been affected by scams, struggle with device setup, or simply want to make technology work better for you, I provide patient, respectful, and thorough support. My approach focuses on making the entire process effortless while ensuring you feel comfortable and confident with your technology.

My diverse experience includes working at Office Max in Davenport as a Control Center Associate, specializing in computer sales and repairs. I've also owned and operated a pawnshop in downtown Muscatine, giving me extensive experience in helping people with various tech issues. Currently, I work with a local corporate carrier selling mobile devices, keeping me up-to-date with the latest technology trends.

What truly sets me apart is my hands-on experience with smart home technology. I live in a fully functional smart home that I personally installed and configured, allowing me to provide practical, real-world solutions for home automation and integration. I understand the frustrations that come with technology and make it my mission to transform those frustrations into positive, empowering experiences.
